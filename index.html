Best hamburger recipe

Recipe for stove or grill

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • egg
  • salt and pepper
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • sesame seed bun
  • condiments: Ketchup, mayo, mustard

Steps

    Preheat cooking serface
    combine egg, salt, pepper, ground beef
    store in cold place until ready to cook
    make sauce by mixing mayo, mustard, ketchup
    cut tomato
    prep buns by either toasting or dressing with sauce, lettuce, tomato
    remove burgers from fridge and cook until medium rare or desired temperature
    Plate and serve
